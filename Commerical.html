Commerical Services

We provide landscape services for commercial, industrial, and homeowner associations. We specialize in all areas of the landscape industry, including landscape architecture, tree and plant care, and lawn and pest control. Our team members are trained to the industry’s highest standards and will ensure that your property is maintained to your exact specifications.

Lawn Maintenance

Our lawn maintenance crews are highly trained to work efficiently with a focus on quality. We understand our customers’ expectations, take pride in our work, and value the importance of attention to details. Our high standards and innovative problem solving help add value to your property

Residential Design and Construction

Our construction team consists of Registered Landscape Architects, Certified Landscape Technicians and highly trained crew members. Whether your goal is to create a new landscape from bare ground, enhance a landscape with a new paver patio, a retaining wall or water feature we can do it. Our services also include Water Wise landscape design and installation. Our team will complete your project on time and on budget.

Plant and Lawn Health Care

Nourishment is critical to sustain healthy lawns, trees and plants. Without the right fertilization and pest control, your lawn and plants have litle defense against harmful pest infestations and/or disease. With over 25 years of experience in the Treasure Valley, we have developed a custom treatment program to meet the needs of your landscape. Our licensed chemical applicators are highly trained and provide the latest Integrated Pest Management practices at competitive prices.

Irrigation Management

Water management is more than just keeping grass green and fixing sprinklers. Our irrigation team is skilled in troubleshooting any problem and performing all repairs. Are staff is trained in working with all types of irrigation controllers and the latest Smart Water Technologies. We believe water is a limited natural resource that we all must use wisely.

Tree Care

Our Certified Arborists will be on your property to ensure that your trees are pruned correctly the first time. We use the best arboricultural practices and equipment available. As a member of the International Society of Arboriculture, we have knowledge of all the latest best Industry practices.

Hydro-seeding and Erosion/Sediment Control

Lawn Co, one of the first hydro-seeding companies in the valley, uses the latest disease resistant and drought tolerant certified seeds along with specifically designed fertilizer, mulch and tackifier. We provide step-by-step instructions to guarantee the success of your new seeding project. We also hold an Idaho Contractors license and a Public Works license and have the ability to handle any size project.
