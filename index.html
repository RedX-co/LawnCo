Lawn Co.

Lawn Co. is a locally owned and operated landscape company in the beautiful Treasure Valley area of Idaho. We have been providing landscape irrigation, design and installation, full service landscape maintenance, hydroseeding, and lawn and tree care since 1985. Whether you are a homeowner trying to protect and maintain your landscape investment or a property management professional needing a full service landscape care provider, Lawn Co. has a solution for you.
